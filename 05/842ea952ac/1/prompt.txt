### Where the lag comes from

The toast fires at step 6, but the sidebar doesn't close until step 9. Two compounding sources of the perceived delay:

- **The toast appears at step 6, but step 7 (`await` on 3 refetched queries) and step 9 (hard-coded `setTimeout(..., 500)` in `page.tsx:86`) both run after the toast.** The 500ms timeout is unconditional and adds visible lag on top of network refetch time.
