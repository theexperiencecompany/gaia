## Overview

Built a workflow you're proud of? Publish it to the GAIA community marketplace so others can find it and put it to use.

## Create Your Workflow

Go to the Workflows page and click **Create Workflow**.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/guides/workflows/workflows_page.webp"
  alt="Workflows page with create button"
/>

Give it a title and description that makes it obvious what it does and who it's for.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/guides/workflows/create.webp"
  alt="Create workflow form"
/>

<Tip>
  Be specific with your title. "Daily Sales Report Generator" tells people
  exactly what they're getting. "Report Generator" tells them almost nothing.
</Tip>

## Publish to Community

Once your workflow is ready and tested, open it from your workflows list. Click the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner and select **Publish Workflow**.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/guides/workflows/publish.webp"
  alt="Publish workflow option"
/>

That's it — your workflow is live on the community marketplace.

## View on Marketplace

Want to see how it looks to everyone else? Select **View on Marketplace** from the same menu.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/guides/workflows/view_marketplace.webp"
  alt="View workflow on marketplace"
/>

<Warning>
  Test your workflow end-to-end before publishing. If it breaks for other users,
  it reflects on you — and them.
</Warning>

## Community Guidelines

To keep the marketplace useful for everyone, published workflows must:

- Solve a real problem or automate a genuinely useful task
- Have titles and descriptions that accurately reflect what they do
- Work reliably as described
- Respect user privacy and data

<Note>
  Workflows that violate these guidelines may be removed from the marketplace.
</Note>
