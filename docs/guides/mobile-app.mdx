## Your AI Assistant, Pocket-Sized

GAIA's mobile app puts your entire AI workspace in your pocket. Chat with GAIA, manage todos, check notifications, and run workflows — all from your phone. Built with React Native and Expo, it runs natively on both iOS and Android.

No laptop required. Ask GAIA to draft an email while you're on the train, check your schedule between meetings, or capture a task before you forget it.

## Get the App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download from your app store">
    Search for **GAIA** on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com) (iOS) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com) (Android). Tap **Install** and wait for the download to finish.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in to your account">
    Open the app and sign in with the same account you use on the web. If you don't have one yet, you can create an account directly from the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable push notifications">
    When prompted, allow notifications. This lets GAIA reach you proactively — reminders, task updates, and workflow results arrive as push notifications even when the app is closed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start chatting">
    That's it. Your conversations, integrations, and settings sync automatically from your web account. Pick up right where you left off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Already use GAIA on the web? Your chat history, connected integrations, and
  preferences carry over instantly — no setup needed.
</Tip>

### More built-in features

Beyond chat and tasks, the mobile app includes:

- **Notes** — capture and edit notes on the fly
- **Reminders** — set time-based reminders with snooze support
- **Calendar** — view your schedule and upcoming events
- **Integrations** — connect and manage your linked apps
- **Memory** — review and manage what GAIA remembers about you
- **Search** — find conversations, tasks, and notes across your workspace
- **Skills** — browse and use GAIA's built-in capabilities

## Voice Input

The voice recorder is built for speed. Hold the microphone button in the chat input to start recording. A waveform animation and elapsed timer show that recording is active.

- **Send** — release the button to send your voice message to GAIA
- **Cancel** — swipe left past the threshold to discard the recording (haptic feedback warns you before it cancels)

<Note>
  The app requests microphone permission the first time you record. You can
  manage this in your device's Settings under the GAIA app.
</Note>

## Push Notifications

GAIA's push notifications keep you in the loop without opening the app. Notifications are registered with the backend per device, so you control which devices receive alerts.

What triggers a notification:

- Reminders you've set
- Background task completions
- Workflow results
- Messages from GAIA that need your attention

<Tip>
  Notifications are managed per device. Signing out automatically unregisters
  your device so you stop receiving alerts.
</Tip>

## Mobile vs. Web vs. Desktop

| Feature | Mobile | Web | Desktop |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI chat | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Voice messages | Yes | No | No |
| Push notifications | Yes | No | Yes |
| File attachments | Photos, camera, documents | All file types | All file types |
| Haptic feedback | Yes | No | No |
| Offline indicator | Yes | No | No |
| Keyboard shortcuts | No | Yes | Yes |

The mobile app prioritizes speed and portability. For longer sessions — complex workflows, multi-tab research, or heavy document work — the web or desktop app gives you more screen space.
