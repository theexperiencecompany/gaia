## Overview

GAIA offers two plans: **Free** and **Pro**. The Free plan gives you full access to core features with modest daily and monthly limits. Pro removes those constraints and unlocks higher limits across every feature.

This guide walks you through checking your current usage, understanding plan differences, upgrading or managing your subscription, and reviewing billing details.

## Check Your Current Usage

Every GAIA feature — chat messages, image generation, web search, workflows, and more — has daily and monthly usage limits tied to your plan. You can monitor all of them in one place.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your avatar in the bottom-left corner and select **Settings**, or navigate directly to **Settings > Usage**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a time period">
    Toggle between **Daily** and **Monthly** views to see how much of each limit you have used.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the progress bars">
    Each feature shows a progress bar with your current usage and the total limit. Green means plenty of headroom. Yellow means you are approaching the cap. Red means you are near or at the limit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Usage counters reset automatically — daily limits reset at midnight UTC, and monthly limits reset on the first of each month.
</Tip>

## Plans and Limits

### Free Plan

The Free plan is yours forever at no cost. It includes every GAIA feature with conservative limits designed to let you evaluate the product before committing.

**Highlights:**

- 200 chat messages per day (5,000/month)
- 10 web searches per day (50/month)
- 5 workflow operations per day (20/month)
- 1 AI image generation per day (2/month)
- 2 deep research sessions per day (10/month)
- 50 todo operations per day (1,000/month)

### Pro Plan

Pro unlocks 25-250x higher limits across every feature, plus priority support and access to private Discord channels.

**Highlights:**

- 3,000 chat messages per day (60,000/month)
- 450 web searches per day (13,500/month)
- 45 workflow operations per day (1,350/month)
- 45 AI image generations per day (1,350/month)
- 20 deep research sessions per day (600/month)
- 1,500 todo operations per day (22,500/month)

Pro is available on monthly or yearly billing. The yearly plan saves 25% compared to paying month-to-month.

<Note>
  Limits apply per feature and per time period. Hitting a daily limit does not affect your monthly allowance for other features — each counter is independent.
</Note>

## Upgrade to Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the pricing modal">
    Go to **Settings > Subscription** and click **View plans**, or navigate to **Settings > Usage** and click **Upgrade Now**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your billing cycle">
    Select **Monthly** or **Yearly**. The yearly option shows the per-month equivalent and the total annual cost.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Click **Get Started** on the Pro card. You will be redirected to a secure checkout page. Enter your payment details and confirm. Discount codes can be applied directly on the checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify activation">
    After payment, GAIA redirects you to a confirmation screen. Your plan updates immediately — head to **Settings > Subscription** to confirm the status shows **Active**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You will receive a welcome email after your Pro subscription activates. Keep an eye on your inbox.
</Tip>

## Manage Your Subscription

Navigate to **Settings > Subscription** to see your current plan at a glance:

- **Plan name and status** — whether your subscription is Active, On Hold, Cancelled, or Expired.
- **Price and billing cycle** — the amount you pay and whether it renews monthly or yearly.
- **Next billing date** — when your next charge is due, with a countdown in days.
- **Subscribed since** — the date you first activated Pro.
- **What's included** — the full list of features bundled with your plan.

### View Plans

Click **View plans** under the Actions section to compare your current plan with available options or to switch billing cycles.

### Cancel Your Subscription

To cancel, contact GAIA support. The cancellation option in Settings is currently routed through the support team to ensure a smooth offboarding experience.

<Warning>
  Cancelling your subscription does not take effect immediately. You retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.
</Warning>

## Billing Details

The Billing section under **Settings > Subscription** shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Billing cycle | Monthly or yearly |
| Next billing date | When the next payment will be charged |
| Last payment | Date of your most recent successful charge |
| Subscribed since | Original subscription start date |
| Subscription ID | A reference ID you can share with support if you need help |

<Note>
  All payments are processed securely through Dodo Payments. GAIA does not store your credit card details.
</Note>

## What Happens When You Hit a Limit

When you reach a daily or monthly cap on any feature, GAIA lets you know inline — right in the conversation. The message tells you which limit was reached and when it resets.

You have two options:

1. **Wait for the reset.** Daily limits clear at midnight UTC. Monthly limits clear on the first of the month.
2. **Upgrade to Pro.** Pro limits are designed so that even heavy users rarely encounter them.

<Tip>
  If you are regularly hitting limits on the Free plan, that is a sign GAIA is saving you meaningful time. Upgrading to Pro pays for itself quickly.
</Tip>
